Power User: {{ user.first_name }} {{ user.last_name }}

DoneCal: Hi Peter, what do you use DoneCal for?

Peter: Tracking how much time I spend on various projects at work. I tag both personal projects and work projects as I'm quite interested in how much time I spend on each in a given week.

I'm still a big fan of Google Calendar but I only use that these days for future events such as upcoming meetings or sharing with my colleagues when I'm away on holiday and such.

DoneCal: How do you enter your events into DoneCal?

Peter: At first I would open the DoneCal website and click to type it in but found it easy to forget to do it. Then I started using the Bookmarklet. Especially if I had just closed an issue on our work issue tracker.
Recently I've started to just use the email reminders. I've got them set up to email me every work day evening at 6pm and now I just reply with hours and the tags on which I've been working.

DoneCal: What in particular do you like about DoneCal?

Peter: That there are numerous and flexible ways to enter things and just that. No rules or restrictions. Within the space of a few months I've already changed my ways of entering events many times. For example, I used to write quite a lot; now I just write the tag with a hash sign. Oh and I like the graphical reporting so I can glance roughly where I put my time in any chosen time interval.
