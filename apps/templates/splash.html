Welcome to DoneCal, first timer!

You can immediately start adding events, change them and export and report on them in many different ways.

1. To get started, click on any day cell and create an event.

2. Tag events by prefixing words with a # or a @ sign.

3. Once added you can move it, resize it, edit it or remove it

There are many other ways to get events in such as by email or API.
See the Help if you ever get stuck.
